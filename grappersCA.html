The CA series grabs are designed so that the magnetic core does not touch or damage the working surface of the metal. In addition, the magnetic grab achieves the greatest efficiency with the smallest possible air gap between the load surface and the gripper. The CA series magnetic grabs can easily handle both flat and round objects and also move large items.
